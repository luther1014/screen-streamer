Screen Streamer

The app is streaming your screen over HTTP as an MJPEG stream.

  • Local preview URL: http://localhost:8080/
  • Stream endpoint: http://localhost:8080/stream
  • On other devices on the same Wi-Fi/LAN, use:
    http://<this-computer-IP>:8080/
    Example: http://192.168.1.50:8080/

Local Preview

If the preview is blank, check:
– That port 8080 is not blocked by a firewall
– That the app has permission to capture the screen (macOS / Wayland)
